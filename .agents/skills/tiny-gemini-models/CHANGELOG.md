## [0.1.0] - 2026-05-10

### Added

- Includes decision rules for choosing the right model per use case.
- Covers `list`, `pricing` sub-commands and `--type`, `--status`, `--json` filters.
- Documents the 2026-10-16 sunset calendar for the Gemini 2.5 family.
